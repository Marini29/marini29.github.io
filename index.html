About me

MSc Design & Engineering based on Milan, Italy

In the 5+ years of experience I have been working in different fields, company sizes, with multidisciplinary and international teams. I have been working mostly in the area of product development, research and innovation. On this time I’ve learned how to create professional 3D models based on design surfaces or sketches, how to create advance finite element models to virtually test the designs either system or component level, and how to build virtual prototyping interfaces for interactive application, to test the user experience all of this with the aim of improve and speed up the product development process.

Another knowledge segments that I acquired along these years are: UX research, parametric modeling, 3D animation, 3D printing, rapid prototyping, and interaction design. All this it has been helpful to deliver projects beyond the main scope, achieving outstanding results.

All this looks like I became a “multi-use or 360 design/engineer” this concept could bring different opinions and perspectives in the design environment. But it was not my intention to become like this, it is the results of all my previous experiences, challenges, and choices that made me build this complete set of skills to deliver better and more competitive results into my projects. This multidisciplinary set of skills is also helping me to understand the possibilities and limitations of each technology, design, and set better the scopes of the project and team needs.

In general, I like what I have been doing because it gave me a mindset of adaptability, self-learning, and confidence. Which at the same time keeps me competitive in today’s working environment that involves design and innovation.

You want to know more about me, please go to the bio page.
