About me

MSc Design & Engineering based on Milan, Italy

In my years of experience, I have been working in different fields, company sizes, inside multidisciplinary and international teams. I focused on the areas of research, performance and innovation inside Product Development.

My workflow uses different design methodologies that systematically adapt to the needs of the project. User center design, double diamond and design thinking are some of the main methods that I use and combine. I have advanced experience in the field of performance engineering, I have spent countless hours understanding high-speed events that can be analyzed and optimized with the use of Finite Element Analysis and Generative and Parametric Design. My second passion is interaction design, where I had the opportunity to create phygital experiences by combining different skills such as rapid prototyping, coding, electronic creation and XR development.

Being passionate about knowledge and technologies makes me become a "360 Desig Engineer". I see myself as a person who loves to learn, adapt and develop in each professional and life experience faced.
This multidisciplinary set of skills is also helping me to envision the possibilities, future and limitations of emerging technologies and design trends. You want to know more about me, please go to the bio page.
